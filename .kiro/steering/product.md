# Product: YouTobia Multimedia

YouTobia Multimedia P.l.C. is an Ethiopian multimedia company with a branded landing site and interactive experience. The site serves as the digital flagship for the YouTobia ecosystem — a holding company with five sub-brands:

- **QenaView** — Streaming & Distribution
- **eTop Production** — Content Production
- **YentaBarsiisaa** — Skills & Education Hub
- **MirXog** — Information Hub
- **EnqoqCash** — Interactive Quiz/Trivia Ecosystem (playable demo on the site)

The site is a single-page marketing experience with scroll-driven 3D animations, a splash loader, dark/light theme toggle, a media hub (blogs/vlogs), and an admin control panel for managing content.

Brand identity is built around a bold red (`#FF1E27`) on deep black/white. The design language is high-contrast, editorial, and architecture-inspired — sharp corners, mono fonts, and no purple gradients or generic AI aesthetics.

The app has cultural roots in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and incorporates Amharic/Ge'ez script and concepts (e.g., "Enqoqlsh" — traditional Ethiopian riddles).
